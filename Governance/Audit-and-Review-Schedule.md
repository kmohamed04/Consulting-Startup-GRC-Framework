# Audit and Review Schedule

This document defines how often the company reviews security controls, policies, risks, and compliance status.

## 1. Policy Review Cycle
- All security policies must be reviewed **annually**.
- Updates must be approved by the Security Lead and Executive Leadership.

## 2. Control Review Cycle
- NIST CSF controls reviewed **quarterly** using the Compliance Checklist.
- CIS Controls reviewed **twice per year**.

## 3. Risk Assessment Schedule
- A full risk assessment must be performed **once per year**.
- High-risk items reviewed monthly until resolved.

## 4. Audit Activities
### Internal Audits
- Conducted **every 6 months** using the Compliance Checklist dashboard.

### External or Customer Audits (if applicable)
- Performed based on contracts or regulatory requirements.
- Evidence stored in the Compliance-Evidence directory.

## 5. Dashboard Review
- Leadership reviews Power BI dashboards **monthly**:
  - Compliance Dashboard
  - Risk Dashboard

## 6. Documenting Results
All audit results must be:
- Logged.
- Assigned an owner.
- Given a remediation deadline.
- Tracked to completion.
